About

A local agent who treats your move like it matters.

I'm John Spilotros, a licensed Idaho real estate salesperson with Keller Williams Boise. I help buyers and sellers across the Treasure Valley make confident decisions, backed by honest advice and real local knowledge.

My story

Real estate, without the runaround.

The Treasure Valley is one of the best places in the country to call home, and helping people land in the right one is genuinely the part of this job I love most. Every client comes in with a different story: a growing family, a first place, a downsize, a fresh start in Idaho.

My approach is simple. I listen first, I tell you the truth even when it isn't what you hoped to hear, and I stay organized so nothing slips through the cracks. You'll never feel like just another transaction, because you aren't one.

When I'm not working, you'll find me out exploring the same neighborhoods, trails, and main streets I help my clients call home.
